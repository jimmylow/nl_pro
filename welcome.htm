About Nyok Lan Group of Companies
Today, with 40 years of track records Nyok Lan Group has grown into a conglomerate with core businesses in undergarments, garments and apparel, cocoa-fibre processiong as well as leisure and hotelier.

The Group core businesses are located mainly in the Teluk Intan township of Perak, West Malaysia. The Group has also setup a raw materials procurement centre in China, to source for competitively-priced raw materials.

The Group currently employs more than 400 employees for its local operations. The Group's undergarments & apparel division produced a wide range of lingeries, casual wear, babies wear. The brassiere collections include seamless bra, convertible bra, microfiber bra, soft-cup bra and even nursing bra, for all type of occasions.

The panties collections also include briefs, bikini, thongs, tanga, hikini, hipster, boxer shorts, boy shorts, low-rise briefs, plus-size briefs, nursing briefs. As a specialist in undergarments design & production, the division also produced girdless, waistband, petticoats, slips, camisoles, corsets, bustier, bridal-type lingeries, baby doll, sleepwears (satin, silk or lycra), accessories like silicone-bra, transparent straps, fancy-bra straps and cups.

The division prides itself in producing export-quality products, in compliance with ISO 9001:2000 Certification, WRAP certification procedures. These products are also marketed as "Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM)" products with export shipments into USA, Europe and Canada.
